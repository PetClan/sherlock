Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after the free trial?

After 14 days, you can choose to subscribe to Standard or Professional. If you don't subscribe, your account will be paused but your data will be retained for 30 days in case you decide to come back.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Shopify admin. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

What is theme history?

Theme history is how far back you can restore your theme files. Standard keeps 7 days of snapshots, while Professional keeps 30 days, giving you more restore points to choose from.

What does Priority Support mean?

Professional plan customers get faster response times and priority handling for support requests. We aim to respond to Priority support within 4 hours during business hours.

Will Sherlock slow down my store?

No. Sherlock runs scans in the background using Shopify's API. It doesn't add any scripts or code to your storefront, so there's zero impact on your store's performance.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We only access theme files and app data necessary for diagnostics. We never access customer data, orders, or payment information. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
